Ingredients:
 - 4 bonless skinless chicken breasts
 - 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
 - 2 eggs
 - 1/2 cup bread crumbs
 - 1/2 teaspoons salt
 - 1 teaspoon paprika
 - 1 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Directions:
 1. Coat chicken breasts in a thin layer of flour.

 2. Mix eggs in medium size bowl. Dunk chicken breasts in batter until they are completely covered.

 3. Mix salt, paprika, and other spices and/or herbs if desired, with breadrumbs.
 	Coat chicken breasts generously in bread crumbs.

 4. Heat frying pan with oil. Once hot, carefully place chicken breasts into pan.
 	Flip chicken occasionally. Remove chicken once the breading turns golden brown.

 5. Let chicken cool for 15 minutes on paper towels in order to absorb excess oil. Enjoy!
